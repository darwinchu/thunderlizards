About Thunder Lizards

We provide credible informations about the study of animal fossils or simply dinosaurs. Our aim is to provide learning environment for children or aspiring paleontologist with a non complicated website that is easy to navigate. After the release of this website to which is only a beta for now, we promise to update the contents weekly for newly find fossils around the globe and new studies too. You may find our sources at the link below the website to check our credibility.

The website is currently under-development and please bear with us. Soon the store will be open and a customer profile to post their own blogs about dinosaurs or anything of the like. We want to hear your voice, if you have free time you may contact us on this page, you can just follow the instructions, hope to hear from you.
